Core Architecture

Price is evaluated across three coordinated axes: session segmentation, higher-timeframe reference levels, and gap inefficiencies. Every element is time-stamped, conditionally extended, and programmatically invalidated when raided.

The result is a unified state machine: time → level → state → extension → invalidation.

Manual drawing is eliminated. Level drift is prevented. Execution timing becomes standardized across days.

Intraday Session Engine

The system tracks Asia, London, New York Morning, Lunch, Afternoon, plus one custom block. Each session is exchange-aligned and time-bounded.

During an active window, rolling highs and lows are calculated dynamically. At session close, the final high and low are frozen and projected forward as horizontal liquidity references.

If price trades through a projected level, extension halts precisely at the violation candle. If untouched, the level persists into the next trading day.

This creates a chronological liquidity map. Untouched highs and lows represent pending liquidity. Raided levels mark structural interaction events.

HOD / LOD State Machine

Daily range tracking unfolds in three phases:

18:00–00:00 NY defines the pre-midnight range. At 00:00, this range freezes as the overnight structure.

From 00:00 onward, live High of Day and Low of Day tracking begins. At 08:30 NY, early expansion is locked as a structural benchmark.

Each freeze event stores a discrete structural layer. Expansion phases remain visible without retroactive distortion.

Higher Timeframe Reference Logic

For each completed day, week, month, quarter, and year:

Final highs and lows are captured. At the new period open, they project forward as liquidity targets.

If raided, extension stops immediately and visual state mutates to completed. If untouched, the level remains active.

Binary liquidity state: pending or executed. No fading. No subjective deletion.

Gap Architecture

NWOG — New Week Opening Gap

Difference between current week open and prior week close defines a rectangular inefficiency zone. The box extends through the entire week and includes midpoint and quarter levels.

Midpoint interaction frequently signals partial rebalance. Full traversal implies structural closure.

RTH Gap

Distance between prior Regular Trading Hours close and 09:30 NY open. Pre-open reference converts into a defined gap box at 09:30, including midpoint.

Used for intraday continuation or reversion context.

ADR Engine

A rolling 5-day average daily range defines statistical expansion boundaries.

Symmetrical projections extend from current day extremes and contract dynamically as price expands. When one boundary is breached, it marks the raid point and removes the opposite projection.

Optional 60–70% threshold reflects realistic exhaustion probabilities rather than full-range expectation.

Custom Time Anchors

Up to six programmable open prices may be defined. At each timestamp, the open is captured and projected as a horizontal bias reference.

These anchors clarify premium/discount alignment and directional conviction relative to structural sweeps.
